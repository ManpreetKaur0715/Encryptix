A landing page is a standalone web page that aims to persuade users to take a specific action, such as signing up for a newsletter, making a purchase, or registering for an event. Users can arrive at landing pages through a variety of digital locations, including pay-per-click ads, social media posts, organic search results, and email campaigns. Landing pages are different from other web pages because they serve a specific purpose for a target audience within a specific moment of an advertising campaign.
